 Welcome to ´Dunkin Divers´, Dublins newest dive club.

 Underwater Sport

Scuba diving is currently the worlds fastest growing sport. Here on the site you can see great images from some of our members previous exploits. You can also find details of all the courses we offer. We have a great range with something to engage divers at all levels of the sport.

 Join us

We are a newly formed dive club located on the West pier of the beautiful fishing harbour of howth. We are currently looking for new members. So if you are either a salty veteran of the deep or a complete novice, come on in and enjoy the site, you might find just what you were looking for.
